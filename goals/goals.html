GOAL 2: Complete the TryHackMe beginner path
Personal Goal
TryHackMe is a site to learn pentesting and hacking, in regards to both hobbyists and career focused people. It teaches the skills from the bottom up on all areas of hacking, from the more stereotypical things such as infiltrating servers to things such as stenography and cryptography. It does this through “rooms”, that contain sets of questions. These questions can both be things related to research or processing the information you’ve just read, to giving some info from the practical task you complete. There are various “paths”, which are structured groups and orders of rooms to complete in order to learn skills in an order. I would be completing the beginner path in order to gain the fundamental skills and knowledge to be able to continue in other areas. I will complete by the semester.
