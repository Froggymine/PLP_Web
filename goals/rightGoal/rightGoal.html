Strategy 2: Breaks
Maintaining Skill and Ability
Taking breaks from playing games every 30-45 minutes helps maitain both physical and metal health, and playing ability. Breaks should be getting away from the computer and getting some sunshine and fresh air. In addition, drinking water regularly is extremly important to maintain physical and mental health.
